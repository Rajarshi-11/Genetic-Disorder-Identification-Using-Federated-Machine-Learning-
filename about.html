About SecureDNA

We're revolutionizing healthcare by combining cutting-edge AI technology with deep genetic insights to empower early and accurate prediction of genetic diseases.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, SecureDNA was born from a collaboration between geneticists, AI researchers, and healthcare professionals united by a shared mission—early detection and personalized prediction of genetic disorders.

What began as a research initiative at the Indian Institute of Genomics has evolved into an intelligent prediction platform assisting patients, clinicians, and researchers across the nation. Our team realized that harnessing genetic data with AI could revolutionize how disorders are detected, predicted, and managed—long before symptoms appear.

Today, we work with medical institutions, research labs, and diagnostic centers to enhance our prediction models and drive innovation in preventive genomics and precision medicine.
