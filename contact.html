Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services.

How reliable is the genetic disorder diagnosis?

Our disease predictions are powered by advanced Federated machine learning algorithms with an accuracy rate of 98%. We analyze multiple genetic markers, patient symptoms, and clinical data to provide the most precise diagnosis and personalized health recommendations for your condition.

How does AI help in genetic disease prediction? system?

AI models analyze large datasets of genetic markers and clinical symptoms to find patterns that indicate specific diseases, improving accuracy and speeding up diagnosis.

How does symptom variability affect genetic disorder diagnosis? issues?

Symptoms of genetic disorders can vary between individuals even with the same mutation, making accurate prediction essential for personalized diagnosis.

Should I get genetic testing if I have symptoms but no family history?

Yes, genetic mutations can also occur spontaneously. Testing can help clarify the cause of symptoms and guide treatment.
